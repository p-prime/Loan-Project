Problem Statement:
The goal is to predict whether a loan application will be approved or rejected based on applicant details such as income, credit history, education, and property area.

Business Objective:
Help financial institutions automate loan approval decisions and reduce risk.




Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was performed to understand relationships between features.

The correlation heatmap shows that Credit_History has the highest positive correlation (~0.54) with Loan_Status, indicating it is the most important factor in loan approval decisions.

Additionally, ApplicantIncome shows a moderate correlation (~0.57) with LoanAmount, suggesting that higher income applicants tend to request higher loan amounts.

The Loan Status distribution indicates that the dataset is slightly imbalanced, with more approved loans (1) than rejected loans (0).
